## 🚨 IF JUDGES ASK QUESTIONS

### "How is this different from weather apps?"
"Weather apps predict. We simulate disaster evolution hour-by-hour and provide AI-powered decision briefs with evacuation routes and resource allocation."

### "What about the ML model?"
"XGBoost trained on 5 years of historical disaster data. Pattern analysis matches current conditions with past events for accurate risk classification."

### "How do you ensure equity?"
"Vulnerability scoring based on demographics (elderly, disabled, low-income). Evacuation routes prioritize vulnerable zones. Resource allocation considers equity metrics."

### "What about accountability?"
"Tamper-proof audit trail using hash chains. Every decision, alert, and parameter change is logged with cryptographic verification."

### "Can it scale?"
"Yes - designed for 10+ coastal districts, 1000+ zones per district, 100+ concurrent users. Redis caching, PostgreSQL with PostGIS for geospatial queries."
